Who We Are

South Africa's Leading
Seafood Supplier

Merlin Fish Market is a premier seafood supplier and import/export company headquartered in South Africa. For over 15 years, we have built an unmatched reputation for delivering the highest-quality fresh and frozen seafood products to discerning clients across the globe.

Our expertise spans the entire seafood supply chain — from sourcing and quality control to cold-chain logistics and last-mile delivery. We partner with fishing communities, processing facilities, and South African buyers to ensure every product meets the strictest food safety and sustainability standards.
